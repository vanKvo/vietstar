FAQ

Search our FAQ for answers to anything you might ask.

Basics

How can I track my shipment?
You can track your shipment using our website under shipping. Navigate to Track Shipment menu which can take your to our tracking portal.
What is the weight limit for my shipment?
The minimum weight for the packages has to be 5 lbs(pounds). There is no limit for the amount of boxes to send.
What items are we not allowed to send?
The list of items not allowed include:
  • Batteries
  • Weapons
  • Knives
  • Military Clothing
  • Cigars
  • Hazardous material
  • Flamable material
Who is in charge of delivering my packages?
Our Partner, Ems local service team will be handling the delivery service directly to the house.
My shipment is damaged, what can I do?
If you have a breakable items, we are not responsible of covering the damages unless the items are insured.
Do I have to pay taxes on the items sent?
Your items are subject to custom fees and duties.

Support

I have lost my items, what do I do?
For lost items, you have to call the local branch located in Vietnam. We have the hotline phone number at:
  • Local Branch: 0931-939-768
  • File a lost item claim> Get Support
Where can I get MST number?
The MST number should be at the top of your receipt that you get from the store.
What if I have a question that isn't answered here?
For anything we haven't covered feel free to reach out to our Contact Us you can call our phone. We're here to help.
Would I get different MST number per every package?
No, you would get one MST per shipment you want to send.

Payments

What methods of payments are supported?
We accept credit cards, cash, check, zelle or venmo.
How do I get a receipt for my purchase?
You'll receive your receipt in store after your payment has been confirmed.
How can I get a refund?
We don't give out refunds for shipments.
